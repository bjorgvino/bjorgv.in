This is a personal homepage. No accounts, no commerce, no third-party advertising.

**Analytics.** The site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is cookieless — nothing is stored on your device. IP addresses are processed transiently by Cloudflare to derive country and network-level aggregates and are not retained or used to build user profiles.

**Hosting.** The site is served through Cloudflare's CDN, which processes request metadata (IP address, user agent) to deliver content and protect the site against abuse.

**Legal basis.** Legitimate interest under GDPR Art. 6(1)(f) — measuring aggregate traffic to a personal homepage.
